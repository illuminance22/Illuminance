Event Description:


I knew that if I failed, I wouldn’t regret that, but I knew the one thing I might regret is not trying. – Jeff Bezos

What if you had the power to revolutionize markets, be the next Elon Musk? 6 years ago, India had 500 Indian startups, today the number stands at 80,000. A whopping 15,400% rise. Entrepreneurs and leaders of tomorrow, we invite you to come participate! Rise to this occasion and test your products’ competency in this simulation of real-world markets. Face off against sharks and pitch as to why your product would be the next big thing. Having a great product doesn’t mean that your customers will see it. Boost creativity and innovate ideas to market your message across. Demonstrate the value of your product. Fill the valleys of need in the market.
Join us in the tank and see if you can get one of the sharks to bite!



Guidelines:


THEME: Climate Change
• The competition will be conducted offline.
• It is a team event. There can be a maximum of three participants in a team.
• The teams should come up with an innovative idea related to the theme given.
• The idea can be about a new product or service.
• There must be a PPT presentation explaining the various aspects of their innovative idea.
• The team must also be able to convince the sharks about the feasibility of their idea.
• Name of the product, Logo, Tag line, SWOT Analysis, Cost-Price Analysis, Market share etc are expected in the presentation.
• The event consists of a Power Point Presentation by the team (max time limit: 10 min) and a Q & A Session with the judges (max time limit: 10 +5 min)
• Duration: Tentative (Based on Registrations)
