Event Description:



A fun filled hunt which will take you on a wild cryptic and interactive journey, and give you the opportunity to use their critical thinking and analytical skills. commit to the moment of discovery, to the thrill of the chase and to the near audible click that happens when you make the shift from confusion to clarity. The satisfaction when, what seemed an impenetrable riddle becomes a clear set of directions. It’s the moment when someone suddenly says, “I’ve got it”.



Guidelines:


• This contest/event is for Grade 9-12 and is a group task.
• A group with 4 participants has to be formed by students from the same institution. Students can be from any grades 9-12. Diversity in grades is encouraged to ensure an equitable competition.
• There will be 10 levels. Questions start with easy in the First level and progress up to the most difficult at the final level. Teams will be given an initial cypher which they have to decrypt and solve, which will lead them to the next question.
• Questions will be based on Mathematics: which could either be a Riddle, a Match, an MCQ, or an activity
• This event would be time based. You may need to roam and search around entire school premises for finding the person to get clue. The teams would be ranked based on time taken for completing all the 10 questions.
• Follow the steps:
o Step 1: All groups will be tested with common question. Whichever group gets to the answer first can move to Level-1. Group will be given a clues and hints to find the person having a next question for Level 2.
o Step 2: Find the person and get the 2nd level question, submit the answer and get another clue to find a person to collect level 3 question.
• In this way, all 10 level questions should be done.
• Whichever the group finds last answer or last level will be announced as winner.
• Duration: 1.5 Hours
